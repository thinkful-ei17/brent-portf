My Story

I'm a full-stack web developer from Gainesville, FL. I spent the last decade travelling the world playing drums and was fortunate enough to play festivals with Metallica, Korn and even Def Leppard. However, I was looking for a change and found a new passion in web development.

I believe that nothing in life thats worth something will come easy and that nothing which comes easy is worth anything. It sounds a bit cliche, I know, but hard work and an ability to quickly learn and adapt has got me this far.

When I'm not learning a new programming language or technology you can find me reading about outerspace or playing drums!
